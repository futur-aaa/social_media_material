artist preview: @alsolquema and #napain create visual code-traits and energetic breakcore beats

alsolquema manufactures portraits of code by writing shader programs and generating visual narratives. She is constantly looking for the expression in the medium, and generating new mediums to express herself.

napain is an audio artist blending ideas and creatively combining complex concepts in performance and works. He works with TidalCycles, instruments, and tradiotional DAW to bring his artistic vision to life.
